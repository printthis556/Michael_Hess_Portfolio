About Me

Hi, I’m Michael Hess. I sit at the intersection of IT, operations, and software—where small improvements add up to big wins. By day, I manage dealership systems and vendors (vAuto, 1Micro, VinPoint, Reynolds), own local IT needs, and keep projects moving. I’ve helped cut vehicle check-in time by ~40% and negotiated vendor contracts to lower service costs by ~15%.

I’m also a software engineering student focused on C/C++, Python, and front-end fundamentals (HTML/CSS/JS). I like building practical tools that reduce manual work, create visibility, and make teams faster. Clean UI, clear data, and measurable outcomes are my thing.

On the creative side, I run a TikTok channel as a side gig for fun. I used to do TikTok content creation full‑time, which sharpened my storytelling, lighting, editing, and analytics chops. These days I keep it lightweight—short, actionable videos on IT fixes, workflow hacks, and behind‑the‑scenes ops, with the occasional 3D‑print/AutoCAD build for variety. It’s a creative outlet that keeps my communication sharp and lets me experiment with new tools while engaging a community that loves practical, no‑fluff tips.

Outside of work, I’m a licensed pilot—aviation taught me to prioritize safety, clarity, and calm problem-solving under pressure. I’m exploring remote-friendly roles where I can combine technical skills, operations experience, and a service-first mindset to deliver results.
